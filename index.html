3 Creating a Journal Entry

This section describes the end-to-end process for creating a new journal entry in the Meridian finance portal. Follow each step in order. Screenshots are provided for reference.

3.1 Navigate to the Journals module
1
Log in to the Meridian Finance Portal
Open your browser and navigate to https://portal.meridianfinance.com. Enter your corporate SSO credentials. You will be directed to the main dashboard after successful authentication.
● 96%
high confidence · AI-generated · unedited
2
Open the Journals module from the left sidebar
From the dashboard, click the Journals icon in the left navigation sidebar. The Journals workspace will load. You should see a list of recent journal entries.
● 62%
low confidence · verify · AI-generated · unedited
3.2 Fill in the required fields
3
Click "Add Journal" and fill in the mandatory fields
At the top-right of the Journals workspace, click the Add Journal button. A form will open with the following mandatory fields: Journal Number, Date, Description, Debit GL Code, Credit GL Code, Amount, and Cost Center.
⚠ Note: The Amount must always be entered as a positive number. Debits and credits are inferred from the account codes selected.
low confidence · verify · AI-generated · unedited
4
Enter the Cost Center from the drop-down
Select the appropriate Cost Center from the drop-down list. Use the search box within the drop-down if the list is long. Ensure the Cost Center matches the department budget owner.
